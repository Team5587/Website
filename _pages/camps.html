Our Summer STEM Camps:

Titan Robotics is offering a variety of STEM themed camps for students from kindergarten through Eighth grade. All of our camps feature content developed and taught by team members. Click on any of the camps below to learn more about them. To register or apply for scholarships, click on the links under the downloads section, and send completed forms to frc5587@gmail.com.

Physics FUNdamentals

Grades K-2
This exciting camp will provide children with a solid foundation in general physics principles and the engineering design process through fun learning and interactive exploration. Camper activities will include creating lava lamps, designing and testing a solution to a water balloon drop, and constructing miniature cars.

Lego Mindstorms

Grades 3-5
Campers will work in teams to build and program LEGO Mindstorms robots to compete in fun challenges. Campers will also learn the engineering design process. This camp is great preparation for FIRST LEGO League!

Rocketry

Grades 3-5
Titan Robotics Rocketry camp is out of this world! Campers will have the opportunity to learn about the science of rockets and space travel through interactive lessons. They will also design and build their own rockets which will be launched at the end of the week!

Intro to Java

Grades 6-8
Go from zero to hero in Java programming! In this course, campers will go from the basics of computer programming in the popular Java language to intermediate level design in one week. Campers will learn through engaging projects and guidance, ending the week with a project of their own invention and skills to kick off a journey into the world of computer science.

Product and Web Design

Grades 6-8
Campers will invent and develop a product of their own design. They will learn to create a model of this product using CAD (computer-aided design) and 3D printing. Then, they will create a web page to showcase their product using basic HTML. This camp is a fun way to utilize STEM skills to learn about the entire product design, development, and marketing process!
