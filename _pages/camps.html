Titan Robotics Rocketry Camp

Team 5587 is running a summer rocketry camp for students in grades 3 - 5, where students will engage with team members to learn about the science of rockets, as well as design, build, and launch their own rockets.

To register, click the link to the registration form here , save the completed form, and email the completed form to the team email at tcwilliamsrobotics@gmail.com.

The price for the camp is $185. Checks should be made payable to "Titan Robotics 5587 Boosters" with "Summer Camp" as the memo. Scholarship applications are available here .
