Our Summer STEM Programs

Titan Robotics offers a variety of STEM-themed summer programs for students from kindergarten through ninth grade. All of our content is developed and taught entirely by team members, and our camps are held at T.C. Williams High School.


Safety is our priority: we are carefully monitoring the situation surrounding COVID-19 and will release any updates regarding our summer camps if anything changes. As of right now, we are preparing for everything to go as planned, and we hope to see you there!

To register for our camps, fill out this form. Our half day camps cost $215 and our full day program costs $325. If registering for two half day camps, the cost is discounted to $400. Payment must be submitted by mailing a check made out to Titan Robotics 5587 Boosters to 3213 Duke Street #824 Alexandria VA 22314.


We offer full or partial need-based scholarships for all of our summer programs! If you would like to apply for a scholarship, simply indicate this on the registration form.
