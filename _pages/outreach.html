2018 Outreach

Del Ray Halloween Parade

It’s a Great Pumpkin, Titan Robotics! This year the team and our robot travelled through Del Ray in full Charlie Brown attire! The team created costumes for both the robot and the team members, and they finished the parade route with a robot demo for the trick-or-treaters!

Meeting with Afghan Girls’ Robotics Team

This past October, Titan Robotics met with the Afghan Dreamers to discuss how girls can make an impact in STEM globally. The Titans demonstrated their robot and gave the Dreamers a tour of the shop. They also discussed the importance of a STEM education, especially for women, throughout the world.

Art on the Avenue

Titan Robotics team members offered so many STEM learning opportunities to the Alexandria community at this event! They enjoyed demonstrating the robot for the kids and letting them each take a turn at driving it themselves. Team members also helped students design their own balloon powered cars and colorful catapults. Little ones even had the opportunity to color robot pictures! It was a fun event for all!
