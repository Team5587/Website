2020 STEM Expo

Save the date for May 30th: Titan Robotics Coalition's First Annual STEM Expo!

Titan Robotics' first ever STEM Expo will feature STEM Exhibitors, a FIRST Lego League Jr. Expo, and FIRST Lego League and FIRST Tech Challenge off-season competitions. It's going to be an exciting day, so mark your calendars for Saturday, May 30th! The event will run from 9am to 6pm at TC Williams High School in Alexandria, VA.


We will send out information about registration for the FLL Jr. Expo and the FLL and FTC off-season competitions shortly.


Safety is our priority: we are carefully monitoring the situation surrounding COVID-19, and will release any updates regarding the STEM expo if anything changes. As of right now, we are preparing for everything to go as planned, and we hope to see you there!
