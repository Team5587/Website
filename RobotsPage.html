2017

This is our robot from the 2017 FRC game Steamworks. Our robot, affectionately named Good Vibes, is a primarly gear and climbing oriented robot. This robot is able to score points in autonomus mode, and retrive fallen gears of the ground. The robot also uses a winch to climb the side of an airship, scoring even more points.

2016

This is our robot from the 2016 FRC game Stronghold.The robot, named Caligula, not only preformed well, but also served as an educational tool at science nights and club fairs. The robot was designed with a low profile, allowing for quick navigation of various obstacles in the arena and shots in both low and high goals.

2015

This is our robot from the 2015 FRC game Recycle Rush. As the first competion that team 5587 participated in, the robot named Christopher demonstrated the skill and dedication of the newly formed team. The robot was able to stack and move totes, lift trash cans, and deposit "litter" into trash cans stacked upon totes.
