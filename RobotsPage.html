Our Robots

This is our robot from the 2016 FRC game Stronghold.The robot, named "Caligula" not only preformed well, but also allowed for the use of the robot as an educational tool at science nights and club fairs. The robots low profile was designed with the intention of being able to quickly navigate obstacles on the course, and to be able to shoot both low and high goals.

This is our robot from the 2015 FRC game Recycle Rush. As the first competion that team 5587 participated in, the robot named "Christopher" demonstrated the skill and dedication of the newly formed team. The robot was able to stack and move totes, lift trash cans, and deposit "liter" (pool noodles) into trash cans stacked upon totes
