Our Team

Team 5587, made of 35+ students and 5+ mentors, design, build and program a robot, either from scratch or from a kit that can be bought from FRC. Our team is given six weeks from “kickoff day”, where we receive the goals of the competition, to build and program the robot. Our team consists of several sub-teams, which are tasked to different aspects of the robot. In addition, There are sub-teams responsible for the fundraising and publicity efforts of the team.
